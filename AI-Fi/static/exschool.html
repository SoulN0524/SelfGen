外匯小學堂

外匯 / Foreign Exchange

外匯是指以一種貨幣交換另一種貨幣的交易，通常用於國際貿易和投資。

Forex refers to the exchange of one currency for another, commonly used in international trade and investment.

匯率 / Exchange Rate

匯率是兩種貨幣之間的交換比率，表示一單位貨幣能夠兌換多少單位的另一種貨幣。

The exchange rate is the ratio at which one currency can be exchanged for another, indicating how much of one currency can be exchanged for a unit of another currency.

點差 / Spread

點差是買入價（Bid）和賣出價（Ask）之間的差額，是交易者需支付的交易成本之一。

The spread is the difference between the bid price and the ask price, representing a transaction cost that traders must pay.

槓桿 / Leverage

槓桿允許交易者使用少量資金控制更大金額的交易，使得潛在收益和風險均被放大。

Leverage allows traders to control larger transactions with a small amount of capital, magnifying both potential profits and risks.

外匯合約 / Forex Contract

外匯合約是一種協議，約定在未來某一時間按照預定匯率交換貨幣。

A forex contract is an agreement to exchange currencies at a predetermined exchange rate at a future date.

貨幣對 / Currency Pair

貨幣對是指在外匯市場中兩種貨幣之間的交換率。常見的貨幣對包括歐元/美元（EUR/USD）、美元/日圓（USD/JPY）等。

A currency pair is the quotation of two different currencies, with the value of one currency being quoted against the other. Common currency pairs include EUR/USD (Euro/US Dollar) and USD/JPY (US Dollar/Japanese Yen).

多頭 / Long Position

多頭是指交易者預期貨幣價格將上漲，並買入該貨幣。交易者希望通過價格上升來賺取利潤。

A long position refers to the buying of a currency with the expectation that its value will rise. Traders aim to profit from the increase in the currency's price.

空頭 / Short Position

空頭是指交易者預期貨幣價格將下跌，並賣出該貨幣。交易者希望通過價格下跌來賺取利潤。

A short position refers to the selling of a currency with the expectation that its value will fall. Traders aim to profit from the decrease in the currency's price.

止損單 / Stop-Loss Order

止損單是一種指令，當市場價格達到指定水平時，自動執行賣出操作，以限制潛在的損失。

A stop-loss order is an order placed with a broker to sell a currency when it reaches a certain price, intended to limit an investor's loss.

限價單 / Limit Order

限價單是一種指令，當市場價格達到指定水平時，自動執行買入或賣出操作，確保交易在預定價格或更好價格執行。

A limit order is an order placed with a broker to buy or sell a currency at a specified price or better, ensuring the trade is executed at the desired price level.

槓桿率 / Leverage Ratio

槓桿率是指交易者使用的資金與實際控制的資金之比率。高槓桿率意味著交易者可以用少量資金控制大額交易，但風險也相應增加。

The leverage ratio refers to the ratio of a trader's funds to the size of the trade they can control. A high leverage ratio means traders can control large trades with a small amount of capital, but it also increases risk.

浮動盈虧 / Unrealized P/L

浮動盈虧是指交易者尚未實現的盈虧，基於當前市場價格與交易價格的差異計算。

Unrealized P/L refers to the profit or loss that a trader has not yet realized, calculated based on the difference between the current market price and the trade price.

實現盈虧 / Realized P/L

實現盈虧是指交易者已經實現的盈虧，基於已完成的交易計算。

Realized P/L refers to the profit or loss that a trader has actually realized, based on completed trades.

技術分析 / Technical Analysis

技術分析是通過分析歷史價格圖表和交易量來預測未來價格走勢的一種方法。

Technical analysis is a method of predicting future price movements by analyzing historical price charts and trading volumes.

基本分析 / Fundamental Analysis

基本分析是通過分析經濟、政治和其他基本因素來預測貨幣價格走勢的一種方法。

Fundamental analysis is a method of predicting currency price movements by analyzing economic, political, and other fundamental factors.
